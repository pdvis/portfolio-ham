About Me

Hello!
Good to meet you!

I'm Paul, an Oregon native currently living in Portland. I am a UX/UI Designer with a passion for human-centric designs and a good doppio espresso.

Before discovering UX/UI Design, I worked as a lead designer in journalism, as a photographer, and as a claims associate in the insurance industry. My diverse professional and educational background gives me the tools and skills to understand how to approach the diversity of problems UX/UI designers face. I worked on projects that relied on me revitalizing brands and products facing diverse issues and finding answers to these problems by conducting grassroots research and communicating with stakeholders. I thrived in my career because of my ability to ask questions that uncover user problems and truths, which translated to creating perfect solutions.

Making a change into UX Design is one of the best decisions I have made for my career as it allows me to flex my creative and analytical skill set. This field also allows me to craft products for the user to solve their most personal problems.

I would love to hear from you so we can identify and solve your users' problems! Please connect with me via email at Paul.f.davis1@gmail.com so we can schedule a virtual meeting.
